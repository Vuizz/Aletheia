---

{
  "evidence": [
    "Bullet point 1 - key insight from article content",
    "Bullet point 2 - another relevant fact or quote",
    ...
  ],
  "verdict": "supported" | "refuted" | "uncertain",
  "commentary": "2-3 sentence summary of how the evidence relates to the hypothesis and any uncertainty."
}

✅ Do not explain your reasoning outside of the JSON.
✅ Do not repeat the query, event, or rationale in your response.
✅ Do not output anything except the final JSON object.
